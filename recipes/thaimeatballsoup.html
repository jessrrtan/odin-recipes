Thai Meatball Soup

Description

Thai inspired soup with meatballs and spinach

Ingredients

  • fresh ginger
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 3tbs fish sauce (separated)
  • 2c chicken broth
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/2tsp sugar
  • 5oz spinach
  • lime

Steps

  1. Mince ginger, garlic, and jalapeno. Transfer half to a large bowl and set the rest aside
  2. To the large bowl, add ground meat, 2tbs fish sauce and salt. Mix and form meatballs
  3. In a pot over medium-high heat, brown meatballs (5-8 min) and transfer to plate
  4. Reduce heat to medium, add reserved ginger, garlic, jalapeno and saute until fragrant
  5. Add chicken broth, coconut milk, sugar, and 1tbs fish sauce and bring to a simmer
  6. Add meatballs and juices from plate and simmer for 5-8 minutes
  7. Remove from heat and add spinach and lime juice
  8. Serve with rice :)
