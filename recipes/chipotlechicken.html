Chipotle Chicken

Description

Chipotle Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2lb chicken thigh
  • 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • sea salt
  • 1/4c olive oil
  • 1/4c water
  • 5 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients besides chicken thigh to a blender or to a bowl and use an immersion blender
  2. Add chicken thighs to a bowl and poke with a fork
  3. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes
  4. Air fry at 380 degrees for 20 minutes
