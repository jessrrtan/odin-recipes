Asian Glazed Chicken

Description

Chicken marinated with Asian flavors and cooked in the air fryer

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thigh
  • 1tbs soy sauce
  • 1/2tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs sesame oil
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • 1tbs Chicken Boullion

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in all of the above ingredients for at least 30min
  2. Cook in air fryer at 380 degrees for 20 minutes (flipping halfway through)
