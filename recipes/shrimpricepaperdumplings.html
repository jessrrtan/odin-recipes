Shrimp Rice Paper Dumblings

Description

Shrimp Rice Paper Dumblings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp
  • Asian chives
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mince chives, garlic, ginger and add to a bowl
  3. Add spices to bowl and mix
  4. Dampen rice paper for ~30sec and wrap filling
  5. Brush neutral oil onto both sides of dumplings
  6. Bake for 7 minutes on each side
