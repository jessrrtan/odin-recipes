Banana Bread

Description

Boonana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2c flour
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1c sugar
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1tsp vanilla
  • 1/2c vegetable oil
  • 1tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl
  3. In a large bowl, combine eggs and sugar then stir in mashed bananas, vanilla, oil, and cinnamon
  4. Stir in dry ingredients into wet
  5. Add chocolate chips if you want
  6. Bake for 20+ minutes
